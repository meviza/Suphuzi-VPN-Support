Your Privacy is Our Foundation

We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. This policy explains how we protect your data and maintain our commitment to zero logs.

Zero Logs

We don't store your browsing history, connection logs, or personal data.

RAM-Only

All data exists only in volatile memory and vanishes when servers restart.

Global Privacy

Jurisdictions that respect privacy and don't require data retention.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike other VPN providers, we have a strict zero-log policy enforced at the infrastructure level:

  • No browsing history: We don't track which websites you visit or what you do online.
  • No connection logs: We don't store your connection timestamps, IP addresses, or server locations.
  • No DNS queries: We don't log which domains you access through our VPN.
  • No bandwidth usage: We don't track how much data you transfer.
  • No personal identity: We don't require your real name, email, or payment details for anonymous use.

2. Information We Minimally Collect

To provide our service, we collect only what's technically necessary:

  • Anonymous usage metrics: Total active connections (no identifiers) for load balancing.
  • Server performance data: CPU and memory usage for infrastructure maintenance.
  • Optional account info: If you create an account, only an anonymous identifier and subscription status.
  • Support requests: Only when you contact us, and only the information you provide.

3. RAM-Only Infrastructure

Our entire infrastructure runs on RAM-only servers:

  • No hard drives: Our servers have no persistent storage devices.
  • Automatic data deletion: All data vanishes instantly when servers restart or are powered off.
  • No forensic recovery: Even with physical access, no data can be recovered from RAM.
  • Regular reboots: Servers automatically reboot to ensure no data persists.

4. Anonymous Payments

We support multiple anonymous payment methods:

  • Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency: Pay via BTCPay Server without revealing your identity.
  • Gift cards: Various prepaid gift cards for completely anonymous access.
  • Cash options: Anonymous cash payment methods in select regions.
  • No financial tracking: We don't store payment details or link them to usage.

5. Legal Protection

We're legally structured to protect your privacy:

  • Privacy-friendly jurisdictions: Operated from countries without mandatory data retention laws.
  • No data to share: We legally cannot share what we don't collect or store.
  • Independent audits: Regular third-party audits verify our zero-log compliance.
  • Transparency reports: We publish transparency reports about government requests.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Right to access: Request any data we have about you (typically none).
  • Right to deletion: We can delete any data, though we don't store personal information.
  • Right to portability: Export any account data you've provided.
  • Right to anonymity: Use our service completely anonymously as a guest.

7. Technical Security

We implement industry-leading security measures:

  • WireGuard® protocol: Modern, encrypted VPN protocol with minimal attack surface.
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy: Every session uses unique encryption keys.
  • DNS leak protection: All DNS queries routed through the encrypted tunnel.
  • Kill Switch: Blocks internet if VPN connection drops unexpectedly.

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may occasionally be updated:

  • No reduction in protection: We will never reduce your privacy protections.
  • Clear notifications: Any changes will be clearly announced in-app and via email.
  • 30-day notice: Major changes receive at least 30 days advance notice.
  • Continuous improvement: Updates may add new protections or clarify existing ones.

Suphuzi VPN

Privacy Policy

Suphuzi VPN keeps the operational data required to run the service, secure accounts, manage subscriptions, and maintain network stability. We do not sell personal data.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

What We Collect

We may store your email address, account identifier, subscription tier, selected traffic profile, active device count, and billing status.

We may also process service diagnostics such as crash information, latency observations, connection state, selected server region, session start and end times, and customer support messages.

What We Do Not Inspect

Suphuzi VPN is built to route and protect traffic, not to read the content of your messages, files, or browsing sessions.

Operational metadata can still exist where it is needed for billing, fraud prevention, abuse controls, service troubleshooting, or legal obligations.

How Data Is Used

We use collected data to authenticate users, enforce plan limits, establish VPN sessions, monitor server health, investigate abuse, and answer support requests.

Payments made through Google Play or the App Store are billed by those stores under their own payment terms and systems.

Sharing and Retention

We share data only with providers that are necessary to operate the service, such as infrastructure, analytics, payment, crash reporting, or support tools.

Account, billing, and security records may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for legal, accounting, operational, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Your Choices

You can request account deletion, change your subscription through the relevant app store, or contact support for privacy questions.

If you cannot complete deletion inside the app, email support@suphuzi.com with the subject Account Deletion Request.

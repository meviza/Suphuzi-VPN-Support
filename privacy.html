Your Privacy is Our Foundation

At Suphuzi VPN, privacy isn't just a feature—it's our core principle. This comprehensive privacy policy outlines our unwavering commitment to protecting your digital rights and maintaining your complete anonymity online.

Zero Logs Policy

We don't store your browsing history, connection logs, or personal data. Ever.

RAM-Only Servers

All data exists only in volatile memory and vanishes when servers restart.

Privacy-Friendly Jurisdiction

Operated from jurisdictions that respect privacy and don't require data retention.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Suphuzi VPN operates under a strict zero-logs policy enforced at the infrastructure level. We fundamentally believe that privacy is a human right, and we've built our entire service around this principle:

  • No browsing history: We never track, store, or monitor which websites you visit, what content you access, or how long you spend online.
  • No connection logs: We don't store your connection timestamps, original IP addresses, server locations, or session durations.
  • No DNS queries: We don't log which domains you access through our VPN, ensuring complete browsing privacy.
  • No bandwidth usage: We don't track how much data you transfer, when you transfer it, or what type of content you access.
  • No personal identity: We don't require your real name, email address, phone number, or payment details for anonymous use.
  • No device fingerprinting: We don't collect device information, browser fingerprints, or unique identifiers.
  • No location tracking: We don't track your physical location, GPS coordinates, or movement patterns.

2. Information We Minimally Collect

To provide our VPN service effectively, we collect only what's technically necessary for operation and security:

  • Anonymous usage metrics: Total active connections (no identifiers) for load balancing and server optimization.
  • Server performance data: CPU usage, memory consumption, and network load for infrastructure maintenance.
  • Optional account information: If you create an account, only an anonymous identifier and subscription status.
  • Support requests: Only when you contact us, and only the information you voluntarily provide.
  • Payment processing: Minimal payment information processed by third-party payment processors, not stored on our servers.
  • App crash reports: Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability, containing no personal information.

3. RAM-Only Infrastructure

Our entire infrastructure runs on RAM-only servers, implementing the highest standard of data privacy:

  • No hard drives: Our servers have no persistent storage devices—no SSDs, no HDDs, no flash storage.
  • Automatic data deletion: All data vanishes instantly when servers restart, lose power, or are shut down.
  • No forensic recovery: Even with physical server access, no data can be recovered from volatile RAM memory.
  • Regular reboots: Servers automatically reboot at regular intervals to ensure no data persistence.
  • Diskless operation: Our servers boot from network images, eliminating any possibility of local data storage.
  • Memory encryption: RAM contents are encrypted when possible to prevent cold boot attacks.

4. Anonymous Payments

We support multiple anonymous payment methods to ensure your financial privacy:

  • Cryptocurrency payments: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero, and other privacy-focused cryptocurrencies via BTCPay Server.
  • No KYC requirements: We don't require identity verification for cryptocurrency payments.
  • Gift cards: Various prepaid gift cards for completely anonymous access without any financial trace.
  • Cash options: Anonymous cash payment methods available in select regions through trusted partners.
  • No payment tracking: We don't store payment details, transaction histories, or link payments to usage patterns.
  • Third-party processors: When using credit cards, payment processing is handled by trusted third parties with PCI compliance.

5. Legal Protection and Jurisdiction

We're legally structured to protect your privacy in multiple ways:

  • Privacy-friendly jurisdictions: Operated from countries without mandatory data retention laws or surveillance alliances.
  • No data to share: We legally cannot share what we don't collect or store—there's literally nothing to hand over.
  • Independent audits: Regular third-party security audits verify our zero-log compliance and infrastructure security.
  • Transparency reports: We publish transparency reports about government requests and our responses.
  • Warrant canary: We maintain a warrant canary system to inform users about government surveillance attempts.
  • Legal challenges: We actively fight against unlawful data requests and surveillance orders.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data and privacy:

  • Right to access: Request any data we have about you (typically none, due to our zero-logs policy).
  • Right to deletion: We can delete any data, though we don't store personal information by default.
  • Right to portability: Export any account data you've voluntarily provided to us.
  • Right to anonymity: Use our service completely anonymously as a guest without any registration.
  • Right to correction: Update or correct any personal information you've provided to us.
  • Right to restriction: Limit how we process any data you've voluntarily shared with us.
  • Right to complaint: File complaints with privacy authorities about our data practices.

7. Technical Security Measures

We implement industry-leading technical security measures to protect your privacy:

  • WireGuard® protocol: Modern, state-of-the-art VPN protocol with minimal attack surface and maximum performance.
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy: Every session uses unique, ephemeral encryption keys that are immediately discarded.
  • DNS leak protection: All DNS queries are routed through the encrypted tunnel to prevent DNS leaks.
  • IPv6 leak protection: Complete IPv6 leak protection to prevent any IP address exposure.
  • Kill Switch: Blocks all internet traffic if the VPN connection drops unexpectedly, preventing data leaks.
  • Split tunneling: Choose which apps use the VPN and which use your regular connection.
  • Obfuscation technology: Advanced obfuscation to bypass VPN blocks and firewalls.
  • Multi-hop connections: Route your traffic through multiple servers for enhanced anonymity.

8. Third-Party Services

We carefully select and audit third-party services to maintain your privacy:

  • Payment processors: PCI-compliant payment processors with strict data protection policies.
  • Email services: Privacy-focused email providers for customer communications.
  • Analytics: We don't use traditional analytics. Any metrics are completely anonymous and aggregated.
  • CDN services: Privacy-focused CDN providers that don't track user behavior.
  • Cloud infrastructure: Privacy-respecting cloud providers with strong security certifications.
  • Security audits: Independent security firms that sign strict confidentiality agreements.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Our data retention policy is simple: we don't retain data:

  • Zero retention period: User data is never stored, so there's no retention period.
  • Immediate deletion: Any data that might be temporarily processed is deleted immediately.
  • Automatic cleanup: Our systems automatically clean any temporary files or cache data.
  • No backups: We don't create backups of user data, as we don't collect it in the first place.
  • Server reboots: Regular server reboots ensure no data persists in memory.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all associated data is immediately removed.

10. International Data Transfers

Our global infrastructure ensures your privacy across borders:

  • Borderless privacy: Your data is protected regardless of which server location you choose.
  • No data export: We don't export or transfer user data between jurisdictions.
  • Local processing: All data processing happens locally on our servers without cross-border transfers.
  • Global compliance: Our privacy practices exceed requirements in all jurisdictions we operate.
  • Server location choice: You can choose which country's server to connect to for additional privacy control.

  • No targeted content: We don't target content to specific age groups or demographics.
  • Parental controls: Parents can use our service to protect their children's online privacy.
  • Educational resources: We provide privacy education resources for all age groups.

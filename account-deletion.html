Account Deletion

Account deletion permanently closes a Suphuzi VPN account and removes or anonymizes data that no longer needs to be retained for legal, billing, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Suphuzi VPN.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open the account area / danger zone.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
  5. If the service requires a recent login, sign in again and retry the deletion flow.

What happens after deletion

Account access is revoked and account-based features are closed.

Data that is no longer required is removed or anonymized as part of the deletion workflow.

Some records may remain where retention is required for billing, tax, fraud-prevention, abuse controls, or security investigations.

If you cannot delete the account in the app

Email support@suphuzi.com from the email address on your account.

Use the subject Account Deletion Request.

Include the platform you use (Android or iPhone) and any error message you saw.

Company Overview

Suphuzi VPN is building a mobile privacy and secure connectivity product on Google Cloud.

This page is intended for platform reviewers, accelerator programs, cloud credit programs, and institutional partners that need a concise overview of the Suphuzi VPN product, operating model, and infrastructure footprint.

Product

Consumer VPN app

Suphuzi VPN is an Android and iOS mobile application focused on encrypted VPN access, controlled server selection, guest access, subscription-based premium plans, and account-based session management.

Infrastructure

Google Cloud backbone

The service uses Google Cloud and Firebase for authentication, control-plane operations, backend APIs, observability, and multi-region infrastructure coordination.

Business model

B2C and B2B

The platform is designed for direct consumer subscriptions as well as future B2B voucher, hospitality, travel, and location-based access distribution models.

Current product scope

  • WireGuard-based VPN sessions
  • Guest access with limited usage allowance
  • Account-based access and subscription tiers
  • Split tunneling, kill switch, DNS protection settings
  • Regional server selection and quality scoring logic
  • In-app legal, support, and account deletion flows

Cloud and backend stack

  • Firebase Authentication for guest and account sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore for app control-plane data
  • Google Cloud Run for backend API workloads
  • Google Compute Engine for VPN node operations
  • Google Play and App Store subscription billing paths
  • Operational monitoring, diagnostics, and service rollout support

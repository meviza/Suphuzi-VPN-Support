Cookie Policy Privacy First

At Suphuzi VPN, we believe in minimal data collection. This policy explains our limited use of cookies and tracking technologies to maintain our zero-logs commitment.

Minimal Cookies

We use only essential cookies for basic functionality. No tracking or advertising cookies.

Privacy Protected

All cookies are privacy-focused and don't collect personal information or browsing behavior.

No Third-Party

We don't use third-party cookies or share data with advertisers or analytics companies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They serve various purposes, but at Suphuzi VPN, we use them minimally and only for essential functionality.

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period
  • First-Party Cookies: Cookies set by our website directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by external services (which we don't use)

2. How We Use Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies strictly necessary for our website to function properly:

  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in to your account (if you choose to create one)
  • Security Cookies: Help protect our website from fraud and abuse
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your display preferences and settings
  • Shopping Cart Cookies: Maintain your cart items during checkout (temporary)

3. Cookies We DON'T Use

Unlike most websites, we explicitly refuse to use certain types of cookies that compromise your privacy:

  • ❌ Analytics Cookies: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • ❌ Advertising Cookies: We don't track you for advertising purposes
  • ❌ Social Media Cookies: We don't embed social media tracking widgets
  • ❌ Cross-Site Tracking: We don't track your activity across other websites
  • ❌ Profiling Cookies: We don't build profiles about your interests or behavior
  • ❌ Third-Party Cookies: We don't allow external services to set cookies on our site

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over cookies on our website:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings
  • Cookie Banner: Our minimal cookie banner lets you accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Private Browsing: Use private/incognito mode to avoid storing cookies
  • Regular Cleanup: Clear your browser cookies regularly for maximum privacy

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality, but your privacy will always be protected.

5. Cookie Duration

Our cookies have different lifespans depending on their purpose:

  • Session Cookies: Expire when you close your browser
  • Authentication Cookies: Last up to 30 days (you can log out anytime)
  • Preference Cookies: Last up to 1 year (you can reset preferences)
  • Security Cookies: Last up to 24 hours for security purposes

6. Your Rights Under KVKK (Turkish Data Protection Law)

Under Turkish KVKK and GDPR regulations, you have the following rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to Information: Know what cookies we use and why
  • Right to Consent: Choose which cookies to accept
  • Right to Withdraw: Change your cookie preferences anytime
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of cookies
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data and cookies

7. International Data Transfers

Some cookies may transfer data outside your country. Here's how we handle this:

  • Minimal Data: We only transfer essential, non-personal data
  • Secure Transfers: All data transfers use encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Privacy Compliant: We comply with international data protection laws
  • Third-Party Processors: We use only privacy-respecting service providers

8. Children and Cookies

We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • No Age Targeting: We don't use cookies to target children
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We don't track children's online behavior
  • Parental Control: Parents can control cookies through browser settings
  • Educational Approach: We provide clear information about cookie use

9. Updates to This Policy

This cookie policy may be updated occasionally:

  • No Reduction in Rights: Updates will never reduce your privacy rights
  • Clear Notifications: Changes will be announced on our website
  • 30-Day Notice: Major changes receive at least 30 days notice
  • Continuous Improvement: Updates may enhance privacy protections

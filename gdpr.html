1. What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a comprehensive data protection law that strengthens and unifies data protection for individuals within the European Union (EU) and the European Economic Area (EEA).

  • Effective Date: May 25, 2018
  • Scope: All EU citizens and residents
  • Global Reach: Applies to any company processing EU citizens' data
  • Fines: Up to €20 million or 4% of global annual turnover
  • Core Principle: Privacy by design and by default

3. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following fundamental rights regarding your personal data:

🔍 Right to Information

Know what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. Transparent information about all processing activities.

📥 Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We'll provide it in a commonly used electronic format.

✏️ Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. We'll update our records promptly.

🗑️ Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Request deletion of your personal data when it's no longer necessary or if you withdraw consent.

⏸️ Right to Restrict Processing

Request restriction of processing your personal data under certain conditions.

📤 Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another service provider.

🚫 Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other legitimate interests.

🤖 Right to Automated Decision Making

Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We only process personal data when we have a valid legal basis under GDPR:

  • 📝 Consent: When you explicitly agree to us processing your data
  • 📋 Contract: When necessary to provide our VPN service to you
  • ⚖️ Legal Obligation: When required by law (rare, due to our privacy-first approach)
  • 🏢 Legitimate Interests: When we have a legitimate business reason that doesn't override your rights
  • 🔒 Vital Interests: To protect your life or in emergency situations

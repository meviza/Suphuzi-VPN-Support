Getting Started

How do I connect to Suphuzi VPN?

Open the app and tap the large power button on the home screen. The app will ask for VPN permission on first use — tap "Allow". The button will turn green when connected. That's it! You can also choose a specific server location by tapping "Servers" from the bottom navigation.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can use Suphuzi VPN as a guest without any account. Simply tap "Continue as Guest" on the login screen. Guest accounts get 1GB of free bandwidth per month. Create an account to sync across devices or manage a subscription.

How do I choose the best server?

Tap "Servers" in the bottom navigation. The app automatically highlights the recommended server based on your location and current load. Green bars indicate low load and fastest speeds. You can also filter by region (Americas, Europe, Asia).

Connection Issues

VPN won't connect — what should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Check your internet connection is working. 2) Try a different server location. 3) Switch protocols in Settings → Protocol (try IKEv2 or OpenVPN). 4) Toggle airplane mode on/off. 5) Restart the app. If none of these work, contact our support team.

My connection keeps dropping. How do I fix it?

Enable Auto-connect in Settings → Connection. This automatically reconnects if the VPN drops. Also enable Kill Switch (Pro plan) to block traffic during reconnection, preventing any data leaks.

My speeds are slow on the VPN. Why?

Free plan speeds are limited to 10 Mbps. Upgrade to Pro for full-speed access. Also try: connecting to a server closer to your location, switching to WireGuard® protocol, or choosing a server with lower load percentage.

Billing & Plans

How do I upgrade to Pro or Ultra?

Go to Settings → Account → Subscription, or tap any locked Pro feature and you'll see the upgrade prompt. Payment is processed securely through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

Can I pay with Bitcoin anonymously?

Yes! Visit suphuzi.com/btcpay on our website to pay with Bitcoin, Ethereum, or USDC via BTCPay Server. No personal information required. After payment, you'll receive a license key to enter in Settings → Redeem Key.

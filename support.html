Support

Use this page for account, billing, server, and app support. Include concrete device and network details when reporting problems.

Primary support email: support@suphuzi.com

What to include

  • Device model and OS version
  • Whether the issue happens on Wi-Fi, mobile data, or both
  • The selected server or region
  • The plan tier or guest mode
  • A screenshot, crash text, or exact error message
  • The approximate time the issue happened

Quick answers

Guest access: You can use Suphuzi VPN without creating an account. Guest access currently includes a 500 MB usage allowance before upgrade is required.

Billing: If you purchased through Google Play or the App Store, subscription management and store refunds are handled through that store.

Connection help: If a connection fails, try a different server, confirm VPN permission is allowed, and tell support whether the problem affects Wi-Fi, mobile data, or both.

Account deletion

If you need to remove your account, use the in-app path Settings → Delete Account.

If you cannot complete that flow, contact support@suphuzi.com from the email address on your account and use the subject Account Deletion Request.

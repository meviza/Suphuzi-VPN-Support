Protocol & Architecture

Currently the world's fastest, most secure, and most private VPN.

WireGuard® uses ChaCha20-Poly1305 — the same cryptography trusted by security agencies worldwide. Combined with Perfect Forward Secrecy, your sessions are mathematically impossible to decrypt, even retroactively.
